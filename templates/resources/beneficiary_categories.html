People with Disabilities (PWDs)

Our Primary Focus

People with disabilities face unique challenges in accessing resources and services. Resource Match prioritizes support for individuals with physical, cognitive, sensory, and developmental disabilities.

How We Help
  • Mobility aids and assistive devices
  • Accessible transportation services
  • Home modification assistance
  • Educational resources and learning aids
  • Employment support and skills training

Women with Medical Conditions

Supporting Women's Health

Resource Match provides support for women dealing with various medical conditions, including fistula, endometriosis, and cancer. These conditions often require specialized care and resources.

Specific Support Areas
  • Medical treatment assistance and funding
  • Post-surgery recovery support
  • Specialized medical supplies
  • Transportation to medical appointments
  • Support groups and counseling services
  • Childcare assistance during treatment

Widows

Supporting Widows in Transition

Widows often face significant emotional, financial, and social challenges after losing their spouse. Resource Match provides comprehensive support to help widows rebuild their lives.

How We Help
  • Financial assistance and emergency funds
  • Housing support and resources
  • Legal aid for estate matters
  • Employment opportunities and job training
  • Childcare assistance and family support
  • Grief counseling and emotional support
Browse Widow Support Resources Support Widow Campaigns

Elderly Citizens

Supporting Our Seniors

Elderly citizens often face challenges related to health, mobility, isolation, and financial security. Resource Match is committed to helping seniors maintain their dignity, independence, and quality of life.

How We Help
  • Home care assistance and daily living support
  • Meal delivery and nutritional support
  • Transportation services for appointments
  • Medication management assistance
  • Home safety modifications
  • Social activities and companionship
